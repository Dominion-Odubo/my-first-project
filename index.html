Course description

As a graphic designer your skill is always in demand by business organisations seeking to create and maintain their visual imageries. Through this course you will learn fundamental design concepts that will enable you to design attractive graphic design concepts that will enable you to design attractive graphic content such as flyers (for online and prints), company brochure, and brand identities. You will learn and master CorelDraw and Adobe Photoshop to enable you create beautiful and professional designs.
